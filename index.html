About ShobhaShringar

Shobha Shringar Jewellers aims to present art made in gold adorned by precious stones and pearls. Each piece is a testimony to its love for traditional crafts, and drive to create unique jewellery. Its forte is to identify the new trends and blend them with antique designs to satisfy and appreciate the modern woman’s desire to have distinctive pieces rooted in tradition and culture.

Designs offered

All the items offered by Shobha Shringar Jewellers have BIS hallmark. Its jewellery is synonymous with purity, craftsmanship and creativity. It has a young team which absorbs the latest trends, which makes it relevant and popular in the ever changing world of jewellery fashion.

Its main collections are in gold and also feature unique combinations in different stones and pearls like turquoise, coral, baroque and Basra and also uncut diamonds. Shobha Shringar Jewellers also offers customised creations to match your vision and personality. Offering various styles from Jadau, to Polki to bridal, and also designer and fusion, you can select anything your heart desire.
